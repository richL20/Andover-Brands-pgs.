About Us

Andover Brands is a collection of brands developed over 30 years and represents 30,000+ individual products. We strive to build a portfolio of brands that bring color, style, comfort and ease to everyday life. Andover Brands designs and manufacture products around the world and supplies them to ecommerce, independent gift, specialty stores and major retailers.
